About VG Search

VG Search is an independent website designed to allow gamers to search for new experiences on a variety of gaming platforms. Gamers can search for a game based on a title or keyword and be presented with Metacritic scores and then at a click of a button be provided with details on the selected game and a handy chart showing the level of recommendation for that game. If you are feeling adventurous you can even select a game from a list of randomly generated games!
